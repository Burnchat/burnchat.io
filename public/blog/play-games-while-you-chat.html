Play Games While You Chat 🎮

We just dropped something fun inside BurnChat — multiplayer games you can play right inside your chat room. No downloads. No accounts. No leaving the conversation. Just tap and play.

First up: Multiplayer Snake 🐍

Classic Snake — but multiplayer. You're playing against everyone in your chat room. Your snake gets a unique color, food spawns on the board, and whoever eats the most wins. It's simple, it's fast, and it's surprisingly competitive.

The game plays right inside your chat window. No popups, no new tabs, no leaving the conversation. The snakes move over your messages. When you're done, hit END and you're back to chatting.

How It Works

Step 1
🎮
Tap the joystick icon in the chat header
Step 2
🐍
Pick Snake from the game menu
Step 3
▶️
Hit GO — your snake appears on screen
Step 4
⌨️
Arrow keys, WASD, or swipe to move

When someone else in the room starts playing, the game restarts fresh so everyone starts on equal ground. The leaderboard tracks scores in real-time.

What makes it different

  • Plays inside the chat — no new window, no new tab
  • Real-time multiplayer over WebSocket — smooth and fast
  • Works on desktop and mobile — keyboard or swipe
  • Each player gets a unique color
  • No accounts needed — same as BurnChat itself
  • Game runs as long as you want — no time limits
  • Live leaderboard showing scores
  • When you leave the room, the game disappears — just like your messages

Still private. Still ephemeral.

This is BurnChat, so you already know the deal. Nothing is stored. Your game sessions don't get saved. Your scores don't persist. When the last player leaves or the room burns, it all vanishes — the chat, the game, everything.

We built this because chat rooms should be fun. Sometimes you're waiting for someone, sometimes the conversation hits a lull, sometimes you just want to race a snake against a stranger. Now you can.

More games are coming 🎮

Snake is just the first drop. That 🎮 button is a game menu — and we're filling it up. We're building more fun multiplayer games that you can play right inside your chat room with anyone who's there.

Think quick rounds, easy to pick up, no installs, no sign-ups — just tap and play with the people you're already chatting with. Puzzle games, reaction games, strategy games — all designed to work inside BurnChat's ephemeral rooms.

Every game we add will follow the same rules: no accounts, no history, no data. When the room burns, the games burn with it. We're not building a gaming platform — we're making chat rooms more fun.

Stay tuned. The 🎮 menu is about to get a lot more interesting.
